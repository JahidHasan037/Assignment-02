To maintain healthy life, We should follow :

1. Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
2. Exercise regularly with a mix of cardio and strength training.
3. Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night.
4. Manage stress through meditation, mindfulness, or deep breathing.
5. Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water daily.
6. Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption.
7. Stay socially connected with friends and family.
8. Schedule regular medical check-ups and screenings.
9. Practice safe behaviors like wearing seatbelts and using sunscreen.
10. Cultivate a positive mindset through gratitude and mindfulness.
